About This Site

The Alliance Water Utility Training Center is a free resource for water and wastewater professionals preparing for certification exams or looking to sharpen their skills.

This site offers interactive calculators, study guides, formula sheets, flashcards, and more — all tailored to meet the needs of utility operators and technicians in the field.

Whether you're studying for your first exam or brushing up on chlorination, hydraulics, or regulations — this platform was built with you in mind.
