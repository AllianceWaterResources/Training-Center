Pipe Dose Calculator Help

Enter the pipe diameter in inches and the pipe length in feet. The calculator estimates the volume of water inside the pipe.

If left blank, hypochlorite strength defaults to 70% and dose defaults to 50 mg/L.

The calculator estimates pipe volume, converts cubic feet to gallons, then calculates pounds of hypochlorite needed for the selected dose and strength.
