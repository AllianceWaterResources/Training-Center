Solids/Time Conversion Help

Enter the mass rate, choose the input mass unit and time unit, then choose the output mass unit and output time unit.

Supported mass units include milligrams, grams, kilograms, ounces, and pounds. Supported time units include seconds, minutes, hours, and days.

The calculator converts the input rate to grams per second first, then converts that value into the selected output mass and time units.
