LSI Calculator Help

Enter temperature, pH, total dissolved solids, calcium hardness, and total alkalinity. TDS, calcium hardness, and alkalinity should be entered in mg/L.

Select whether calcium hardness is entered as Ca²⁺ or CaCO₃. If calcium is entered as CaCO₃, the calculator converts it internally before calculating the index.

Positive values generally indicate scale-forming tendency, negative values indicate undersaturation, and values near zero indicate approximate equilibrium.
