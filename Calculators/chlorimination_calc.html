Chloramination Calculator Help

Enter the water amount or flow and the free chlorine residual that needs ammonia added for chloramination.

If left blank, the calculator uses a target Cl₂:NH₃ ratio of 4.5:1, 19% ammonia solution strength, and 7.74 lb/gal solution weight.

The calculator estimates the ammonia dose from NH₃ dose = free Cl₂ ÷ ratio, converts that dose to pounds per day, then converts pounds per day into gallons per day of ammonia solution.
